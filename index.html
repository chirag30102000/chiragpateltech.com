About Chirag Patel

Stevie® Award Winner — Technology Excellence

Chirag Patel (CP) is Head of Europe – AI & Digital Transformation at Hidden Brains, a global technology consulting firm, based in Munich, Germany. He leads Hidden Brains' European operations, working with enterprises across the DACH region, BENELUX, and the Nordics.

Chirag partners with mid-market and enterprise organizations across manufacturing, logistics, retail, and professional services to design and implement practical AI adoption strategies and digital transformation roadmaps. His work focuses on helping organizations move from AI experimentation to production-ready execution, delivering measurable operational and business outcomes.

With a background spanning software engineering, product strategy, and business development, he brings a full-stack perspective to technology consulting — bridging the gap between business strategy and technical execution. His focus areas include enterprise AI adoption, generative AI strategy, legacy system modernization, data readiness, and end-to-end digital transformation programs.

Chirag is an active voice in the European technology ecosystem, contributing insights on AI, digital transformation, and the future of enterprise technology. He speaks at industry forums and engages with leadership teams navigating the intersection of strategy, innovation, and execution.
